Simple Omellete Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.this classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection optionally.

Preparation Time

  • Total : approximately 10 minutes.
  • Preparation :5 minutes.
  • Cooking :5 minutes.

Ingredents

  • 2,3 large eggs
  • salt to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional:cheese,vegetable

Instruction

  1. Beat eggs: in a bowl,beat the eggs with the pinch of pepper and salt.
  2. heat the pan:heat the pan and add some oil or butter.
  3. Cook omelette:once the butter is melted,pour in eggs.
  4. Add fillings:add some cheese or vegetables for better taste.
  5. Fold and serve:once a filling done then serve on plate.
  6. enjoy:enjoy the taste of omlette.
